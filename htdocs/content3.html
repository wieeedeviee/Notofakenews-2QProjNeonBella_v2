Prevalence of Fake News in the Philippines

With the Philippines being the top social media user in 2020 and one of the most social media-obsessed countries in the world (Mendiola & Varona, 2020), it is with no doubt that its people is vulnerable to the lies brought about by this technology.

Below are resources from news websites that discuss the prevalence of fake news in Philippine society and how it affected people’s mindsets, everyday lives, the pandemic, the elections, and historical accounts.
