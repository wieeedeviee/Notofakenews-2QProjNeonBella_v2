What is fake news?

Fake news is false and misleading information disguised or appearing as factual and verified news. Said information is usually appealing and seemingly contains reliable information – appearing as “normal news,” leading to its belief. Fake news aims to manipulate one’s perception or opinion on particular topics, e.g., politics, real-life events, and statements, or create biases among persons.

Disinformation

If misinformation is incorrect information not necessarily intended to be false, disinformation is intended misinformation. Disinformation therefore is misinformation deliberately disseminated while the source knows it is verified untrue. Disinformation aims to deceive the audience on purpose (Budgar, 2022) as propaganda against a rival for example.
